The Best Movies According to Amber.

My top 3 movies of all time.

Barbie Princess Charm School

Its a story about a girl named Blair who lived a normal life of a waitress supporting her family,until one day her sister enters her in to the charm school where after numerous adventures she finds out that she was the daughter of the King and the Queen who were killed in an accident by the intercharge of charm school so that she could make her daughter Delancy the Queen and rule over the Kingdom,but with the help of her friends and Delancy,Blair unravelled the mystery and was crowned as the new Queen.

Barbie Rock 'N Royals

In this movie, a modern princess's world is turned upside down when she switches places with Erika, a famous rockstar. Two different worlds collide when a mix-up send princess Courtney to Camp Pop and Erika to Camp Royalty.

Barbie & her Sisters in a Pony tale

At a fun-filled riding academy in the Alps, Barbie and her sisters go on the vacation of a lifetime and Barbie finds the horse of her dreams.
